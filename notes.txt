Pages->_Layout.cshtml
    - Defines the default html structure of the webpage
    - Contains the head and body tags
    - RenderBody() refers to Shared->MainLayout.razor
    
Shared->MainLayout.razor
    - Inherits from LayoutComponentBase (Pages->_Layout.cshtml)
    - Defines the shared layout of the webpage
    - @Body refers to the content of the page (Pages folder)
